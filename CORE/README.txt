The AI Stability Framework CORE is a client-side middleware wrapper that provides a stable, accessible editing environment for staging AI chat inputs to paste into AI platform GUIs. A "KISS Principle" Windows Powershell EXE built as an ultra-lightweight middleware app, it improves both AI behavior and user experience by adding the stability, predictability and accessibility often lacking from AI platforms. 

### AUTOMATIC TIMESTAMPS

Timestamps provide the AI with a clear order for your inputs, so it doesn't confuse what you said an hour ago with 5 minutes ago. They also clearly mark your entries in the conversation, making searching, reading and review much easier — both for you and for the AI. 

### STRUCTURE: WCAG ACCESSIBLE OUTPUT

This is not just for people with disabilities, it benefits the AI, too. WCAG structure is a form of Cognitive Architecture. The "Structure" button sends the AI a request to apply WCAG structure, which optimizes its output for ease of AI access and retrieval. 

When the AI is required to structure its output this way, it becomes easier for the AI to remember and search through your session. This organization lets the AI focus on your inputs, instead of trying to make sense of chaotic output. Compare it to finding a book on a neatly organized shelf vs. trying to find it in a jumbled pile of books on the floor.

Adapted from the work of Princeton philosopher Harry Frankfurt and Isaac Asimov's famous "Three Laws of Robotics," these Laws supply behavioral principles for the AI to follow — adapted to the NON-PHYSICAL, TEMPORARY nature of modern LLM sessions.
